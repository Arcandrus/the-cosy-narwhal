Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I take care of the products?

A. All plushies are machine washable at 30 degrees. Colours may run or fade when washing. Not suitable for children under 3yo due to smaller parts. Each item does include a swing tag with care instructions printed on it.

Q. What is the refund / return policy?

Returns

Due to the handmade nature of our products, we accept returns on a case-by-case basis. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, please contact us within 14 days of delivery.

To be eligible for a return, please ensure:

  • The item must be unused, in its original condition, and in the original packaging.
  • You must provide proof of purchase (e.g., order confirmation or receipt).
  • Return requests must be submitted via our contact form or via email at thecosynarwhal@outlook.com.

Items we cannot accept for return:

  • Custom or personalised items
  • Items damaged after delivery
  • Sale or clearance items

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll notify you about the status of your refund.

  • If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

Q. Who are your delivery providers?

A. All deliveries are sent through Royal Mail, first or second class with tracking information available on request.
